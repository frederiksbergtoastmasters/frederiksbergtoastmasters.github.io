Meeting Agenda

Most meetings follow the following steps (roles):

  1. Meeting starts at 11:00
  2. Introduction (president)
  3. Word of the Day (grammarian)
  4. Program Description (toastmaster)
  5. Optional Introductory Round (toastmaster)
  6. Prepared speeches (up to 4 speakers)
  7. One Minute Feedback (all attendees, after each speaker)
  8. Voting for Best Prepared Speech (all attendees, ballot counter)
  9. Break
  10. Table Topics (topicsmaster)
  11. Voting for Best Table Topics Speech (all attendees, ballot counter)
  12. Prepared speech evaluations (up to 4 evaluators, one per speaker)
  13. Timer Report (timer)
  14. Ah-Counter Report (ah-counter)
  15. Grammarian Report (grammarian)
  16. General Evaluator report (general evaluator)
  17. Club Business & Awards (president)
  18. Meeting ends at 13:00
