Join Procedure

If you have never been a Toastmaster, you need to Join as a New Member.

We will help you follow this procedure:

  1. Review and fill the membership form by specifying New Member and your desired start date
  2. Review and fill the global data protection (GDPR) consent form and review our privacy
  3. Send the two documents above to frederiksberg@toastmasters.dk
  4. Receive by email the membership form signed by one of our board members
  5. Pay the fee through one of the payment options (specify your full name!)
  6. Receive by email a confirmation for your membership and your new Toastmasters account
  7. Login onto Toastmasters International by following the instructions in your confirmation email
  8. Choose your path on pathways
  9. Sign up for the next meeting on EasySpeak
  10. Request your first Ice Breaker speech on EasySpeak

If you were a Toastmasters member in the past, additional steps are needed. Contact Us to Resume Your Membership and we will help you (depending on your situation).
