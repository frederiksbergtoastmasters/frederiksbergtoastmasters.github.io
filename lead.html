Lead

Take a Club Officer Role!

We run elections every half year in November-December and May-June. Election is made by secret universal vote from the people present at the meeting on the election day.

Any member can apply for a role. Members who have shown leadership by taking meeting roles and in any other ways contributed to the success of our club are, naturally, more likely to get votes and win the elections for their desired role!

Download Board Roles Manuals and then Contact the Board to Express Intent and specify your desired role.
